Lady Skollie

Lady Skollie is an artist that focuses on the concepts of gender and consent in South Africa.

Through her pseudonym and artistic personality,Lady Skollie aims to create an angency in which she communicates themes that are difficult to directly speak about.The term Skollie is a historical term that orginages in the Dutch Colonized South Africa.

Awards:She won the 2020:10th annual FNB Art Price.

Nelson Makamo

Nelson Makamo is a Johannesburg based artist.He was born in 1982, in a town called Modimolle in South Africa's Limpopo province.

Nelsons work is strongly influenced by the candid innocents of children.He is particularly drawn to the children in rural South Africa.He belives that they embody the peace and harmony we all strive for in life, the search for eternal joy lies in the child within us all.

Awards:Vanguard Award from Harvard University.

Daniella Mooney

Daniella Mooney is an artist that works across multiple mediums such as painting, sculpture and installation.She is currently based in Berlin

She studied a BA Fine Art at Michaelis School of Fine Art, at the University of Cape Town South Africa.

Awards:Sasol New Signatures, Simon Gerson Prize , Studiowork Medal.
